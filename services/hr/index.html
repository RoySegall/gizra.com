Staffing & Recruitment Industry

Recruitment is a challenging venture. To be successful in today’s talent-driven hiring climates, recruiters need to be better marketers and offer a high quality candidate experience through their website. We understand your business and can create a website that supports your business goals.

User Experience and Design

Increase the value of your brand with a polished professional look that engages users. A unique website design built fresh for each client (we don’t believe in templates) allows you to stands out and provide a great candidate experience with the ultimate goal of lead generation. Broaden your audience with a fully responsive website designed to be compatible with a range of browsing and navigational systems that includes the important mobile web traffic.

Job Board

As the go-to resource for job seekers, job board posting is a critical channel in recruitment marketing to get the best talent possible.

  • Advanced search (also accessible by smartphones): jobs by type, industry, proximity and keyword.
  • Job seekers can quickly apply online and submit their resumes. Mobile optimized with one-click apply by using "Apply with LinkedIn" and "Apply with Indeed".
  • Intelligent job alerts when new jobs are posted.
  • Easy to use job editor with fast re-posting features.
  • Social media recruiting with automatic re-posting of your jobs to Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn.

SEO Optimization

Your site coding is search engine friendly, implements tags, and keeps the job search in your domain. Job posts include optimization of URLs (also know as "clean URLs"), page titles, and meta descriptions based on job title and geographic location.

Content Management

Capabilities to support blogs, articles, salary survey data or any other materials relevant to your target candidate.
